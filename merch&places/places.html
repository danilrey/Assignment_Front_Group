Places dedicated to the sponge

Krusty Krab Fan Cafe, Moscow, Old Arbat Street

This is a fan cafe based on the restaurant of the same name from the animated series "SpongeBob SquarePants".
The cafe was opened in 2016 on the site of the "Crazy Toilet" cafe.
Unfortunately, the cafe closed for unknown reasons.

Enjoy the Krusty Krab atmosphere at this unique cafe in Moscow.
