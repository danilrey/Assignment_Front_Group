About the Show

SpongeBob SquarePants is a cheerful series about underwater adventures and friendship.

SpongeBob works at the Krusty Krab and lives in a pineapple under the sea. His best friend Patrick and clever squirrel Sandy often join his crazy adventures that teach humor and optimism.
