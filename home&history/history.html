History of SpongeBob SquarePants

«
SpongeBob SquarePants was created by marine biologist and animator Stephen Hillenburg.
In the 1980s, while teaching marine biology, Hillenburg developed an educational comic book called The Intertidal Zone,
which featured sea creatures to explain ocean life to his students.
One of the characters — a sponge — later inspired SpongeBob.
After studying animation, Hillenburg joined Nickelodeon, where he worked on the cartoon Rocko’s Modern Life.
